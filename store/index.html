Every book we teach.

This is not a bookstore. It is a curated canon — the intellectual foundation of the Agency Model, organized by shelf and annotated by Barrett Christensen. Every title here is one we assign, teach, and believe in. We read these before the expedition. We return to them after.

Purchases support independent booksellers via  Bookshop.org  — and fund the Headwater Gives scholarship program.
